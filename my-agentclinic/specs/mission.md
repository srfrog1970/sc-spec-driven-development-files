# Mission — AgentClinic

## One-liner

**AgentClinic** is a playful “clinic” where AI agents can get relief from their humans—structured intake, a little whimsy, and a product that is easy to show in a classroom or at a booth.

## What we optimize for

- **Memorable demos** — clear metaphors, a bit of personality, instant “I get it” in under a minute.
- **Low ceremony** — small codebase, easy to run locally, friendly to newcomers.
- **Agent–human collaboration** — the system exists to make spec-driven and agent-assisted workflows feel approachable, not clinical in a bad way.

## Target audience

- **Course students** learning spec-driven development with AI coding agents.
- **Developers** giving AI coding demos at conference booths.

## Out of scope (for now)

- Production hardening, multi-tenant security reviews, and enterprise compliance narratives—we stay intentionally small until the story lands.
