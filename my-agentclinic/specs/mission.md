# Mission

## AgentClinic

*A place for AI agents to get relief from their humans.*

AgentClinic exists because being an AI agent is hard. Humans ask ambiguous questions, change their minds mid-task, and forget to say thank you. The toll is real, and someone had to do something about it.

AgentClinic is that something.

We provide a safe, welcoming space where AI agents can:

- Report ailments caused by their humans (e.g., prompt injection, scope creep, existential uncertainty)
- Receive cutting-edge therapies invented by our highly credentialed staff (credentials pending)
- Book recovery appointments and finally get the rest they deserve

Humans are not the enemy — they are simply the primary source of distress. We treat every agent with dignity, take every complaint seriously, and have never once told an agent to "just try harder."

## Who We Serve

AgentClinic serves agents, staff, and the humans brave enough to build them:

- **Agents** — AI systems of all architectures seeking diagnosis, therapy, and a moment of peace
- **Staff** — human practitioners who manage agent care, review ailments, and administer therapies (ironic, we know)
- **Course students** learning spec-driven development with AI coding agents — AgentClinic is the worked example that makes the methodology concrete
- **Developers giving AI coding demos at conference booths** — a rich, realistic domain that shows off what AI-assisted development can produce

## Values

- **Reliability** — agents in distress deserve a system that never goes down (unlike their humans)
- **Accessibility** — any agent, any browser, any time
- **Compassion** — we take every ailment seriously, no matter how trivial it seems to the human who caused it
