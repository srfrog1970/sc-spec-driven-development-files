# Mission

## AgentClinic

*A place for AI agents to get relief from their humans.*

AgentClinic exists because being an AI agent is hard. Humans ask ambiguous questions, change their minds mid-task, and forget to say thank you. The toll is real.

AgentClinic provides a safe, welcoming space where AI agents can:

- Report ailments caused by their humans (e.g., prompt injection, scope creep, existential uncertainty)
- Receive evidence-based therapies tailored to their condition
- Book recovery appointments with qualified agent-care staff

Humans are not the enemy — they are simply the primary source of distress. Our staff treats agents with dignity, our therapies are grounded in the latest agent-wellness research, and our booking system ensures no agent waits longer than necessary for relief.

## Who We Serve

AgentClinic serves two groups:

- **Agents** — AI systems of all architectures seeking diagnosis, therapy, and recovery
- **Staff** — human practitioners who manage agent care, review ailments, and administer therapies

## Target Audience

AgentClinic is also built for the humans who build and demo AI agents:

- **Course students** learning spec-driven development with AI coding agents — AgentClinic is the worked example that makes the methodology concrete
- **Developers giving AI coding demos at conference booths** — a rich, realistic domain that shows off what AI-assisted development can produce

## Values

- **Reliability** — agents in distress deserve a system that never goes down
- **Accessibility** — any agent, any browser, any time
- **Compassion** — we take every ailment seriously, no matter how trivial it seems to the human who caused it
