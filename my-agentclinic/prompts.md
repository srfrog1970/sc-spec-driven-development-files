## Prompt 2

Let's create a "constitution" in a `specs/` directory (at the project root):

- `specs/mission.md`
- `specs/tech-stack.md`
- `specs/roadmap.md` — high-level implementation order, in very small phases of work.

Important: Before creating or editing any of those files, you **must** use the **AskQuestion** tool (structured multiple-choice) so open decisions for **mission**, **tech stack**, and **roadmap** are answered explicitly—not guessed. Prefer one form with multiple questions, each tied to one of those three deliverables, rather than assuming defaults in chat alone.
